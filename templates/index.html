12 Month LLM in International Law Diplomacy and Security

The University of Gold Coast (UGC) stands at the forefront of higher education in Ghana – a faith-driven, academically rigorous, and globally oriented Christian university accredited to deliver exceptional undergraduate and postgraduate programs. UGC traces its distinguished roots to the Graduate School of Governance and Leadership (GSGL), celebrated for shaping principled public and corporate leaders. Evolving from the African Centre for Leadership and Human Resource Development (AFRILEAD) in 2004, to the renowned Accra Business School (ABS), and now a fully chartered university, UGC is synonymous with academic excellence, ethical leadership, and transformative education. At UGC, academic rigor meets moral character. Here, you’ll discover a curriculum benchmarked against international best practices, delivered by leading academics and seasoned industry experts. With a mission to develop visionary leaders deeply rooted in Christian values, UGC prepares graduates to serve with integrity and lead global transformation.
